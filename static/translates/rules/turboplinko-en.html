How to Play?

  • Choose the Risk Level and number of Pins
  • Set how many Disks you want to drop
  • Press the "Drop Disks" button!

Game Details

Place Bet

Enter a valid amount in the field “Disk cost”.
Choose the number of Rows. This affects game odds and bet multiplier: the more rows, the bigger multiplier along the edges of the game field, but the odds in the center are less
Choose Risk level: the higher the risk level, the bigger multiplier along the edges of the game field, but the odds in the center are less
Set Disks amount  from 5 to 10
Press the “Drop Disks” button

Gameplay

Dropped-out disks are bouncing off of the pins, until they reach the bottom of the game field, landing in one of the cells with corresponding multiplier
Multiplier applicable = (sum of the coefficients / number of disks)
Payout = multiplier applicable x bet price

RTP

Game relies on a coefficient called 'RTP' (Return to Player), which is the statistical average of payouts over billions of rounds.
The minimum RTP for Turbo Plinko is 96.73%

Settings

Click the gear icon in the top right corner of the screen to open the settings menu. In Settings you can:

  • сhange the nickname
  • view bet limits: Minimum bet, Maximum bet, Maximum Profit
  • turn on/off sounds, music & animation
  • view a list of your recent bets 

Disconnection Policy

We prioritize the security and satisfaction of our players, even in the event of unexpected internet connection interruptions. Here's how we handle such situations:

  • If a bet is placed after a disconnection, it will not be sent to the server, no funds will be deducted, and the game will not proceed.
  • If a disconnection occurs during an active game, the game state on the server will remain unchanged. Players will be able to resume the game once the connection is restored.
